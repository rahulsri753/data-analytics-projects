## Superstore Sales Dashboard | Power BI

### Project Overview:

This project presents an interactive sales performance and time-series analysis dashboard built using Power BI on the Superstore dataset.

The dashboard focuses on sales trends over time, profitability, customer behavior, and geographic performance to support data-driven decision making.

### Business Objectives:

Analyze overall sales and profit trends over time

Perform time series analysis to identify seasonality and growth patterns

Track year-over-year (YoY) sales and profit performance

Identify top-performing categories, sub-categories, and states

Evaluate the impact of shipping mode, payment mode, and customer segments

### Key KPIs:

Total Sales: 1.57M

Total Profit: 175.26K

Total Quantity Sold: 22K

Total Orders: 5901

Average Discount: 3.93%

Average Shipping Days: 79.83

### Dashboard Insights:

Time Series Analysis shows a steady upward sales trend with clear monthly fluctuations

YoY comparison (2019 vs 2020) indicates consistent growth in both sales and profit

Q4 months (Oct–Dec) record peak sales and profitability

Office Supplies remains the top revenue-generating category

Standard Class shipping contributes the highest sales volume

California and New York lead in state-wise sales performance

### Time Series Analysis Highlights:

Daily and monthly sales trends analyzed using Order Date

Identified seasonality patterns and sales spikes

Compared historical sales performance to recent periods

Enabled trend-based insights for forecasting and planning

### Tools & Technologies Used:

Power BI – Dashboarding, DAX measures, time intelligence

Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

Excel / CSV Dataset – Source data
