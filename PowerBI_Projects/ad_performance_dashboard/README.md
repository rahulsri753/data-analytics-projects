# Meta Ad Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## Objective
To analyze Meta (Facebook & Instagram) advertising performance across the full marketing funnel and identify opportunities to improve engagement, conversions, and overall campaign effectiveness.

## Business Problem
Digital ad campaigns often generate high impressions and engagement, but conversions may lag due to audience targeting, timing, or ad format inefficiencies. This project focuses on identifying funnel drop-offs and optimizing ad performance using data-driven insights.

## Key KPIs
- Impressions  
- Clicks  
- Click Through Rate (CTR)  
- Engagements (Likes, Shares, Comments)  
- Purchases  
- Conversion Rate  
- Purchase Rate  
- Cost Metrics (Average & Total Spend)

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- DAX  
- Excel (data source)

## Dashboard Analysis & Insights
- Analyzed the end-to-end ad funnel from impressions to purchases.
- Observed a strong CTR of 11.76%, indicating effective creatives and targeting.
- Identified a conversion drop-off after engagement, highlighting potential landing page or funnel optimization opportunities.
- Compared ad type performance (Carousel, Image, Stories, Video) and found Stories and Video ads delivering higher engagement and conversion rates.
- Performed time-based analysis using hourly, weekly, and monthly trends to identify high-performing time slots.
- Conducted demographic analysis by age and gender to identify the most responsive audience segments.
- Analyzed geographic performance using country-level impressions to identify high-reach markets.
- Enabled dynamic filtering using Month, Campaign Name, Target Interests, and metric selection slicers.

## Key Outcomes
- Identified high-performing ad formats and audience segments.
- Highlighted funnel stages requiring conversion rate optimization (CRO).
- Provided actionable insights to improve ad scheduling, targeting, and budget allocation.
- Enabled interactive and data-driven campaign performance monitoring.
